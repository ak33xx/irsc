IRSC DTU

We carry the torch lit by IRSC

The Indian Road Safety Campaign was started as a project under the IIT Delhi chapter of National Service Scheme with help of Transport Research and Injury Prevention Program ( set up under Government of India) when few students of IIT Delhi lost their precious life in a road accident just after being placed in well reputed firms.


IRSC DTU chapter is an effort to carry forward the ideology of road safety.We work on various domains of road safety such as Technical, Legal, Medical etc. We work both independently along with constant collaboration with various NGOs and authorities.
